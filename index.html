I am currently working at Fannie Mae. My main duty is to Support, develop, assess, and execute statistical, mathematical, economic, and financial models for business decision making, risk assessment, financial valuation, and performance measurement in the area of single family mortgage finance business. Prior to joining Fannie Mae, I had experience working as an STC at the World Bank. I graduated from the PhD program in Economics at The George Washinton University in 2016.
Some of my projects at Fannie Mae are listed below. I am also interested in academic research, my publication records are shown below.
Interests. Real Estate Economics, Urban Economics, Applied Micro

Projects

  • COVID Forbearance Score Model This model is used to help servicers scoring mortgage loans that are currently in Forbearance due to COVID-pandemic. The model output is a score value that rank-orders the loans by its potential likelihood of exiting the Forbearance plan.
  • COVID Forbearance takeup forecast model This model is used to forecast future Forbearance takeup rates using modern Machine Learning (ML) approaches. The model output is loan level forecasted FP loans at weekly frequency and can be up to any arbirary forecast length specified by users.
